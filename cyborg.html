CYBORG Event by Team ABHEDYA at VBIT

Team ABHEDYA recently organized an event named CYBORG, which aimed to educate the students of VBIT about the importance of cybersecurity. The event was a resounding success, with over 120 students in attendance. The event was designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of cybersecurity, including the types of cyber-attacks, their impact, and how to prevent them. The event featured distinguished guest speakers who shared their expertise in cybersecurity with the participants.

The event was structured in a way that allowed the students to have a deep understanding of cybersecurity. The sessions were interactive, and the students had the opportunity to ask questions and clarify their doubts. The practical sessions were particularly helpful as the students were able to see the real-time impact of cyber-attacks and how they can be prevented. The feedback from the students was overwhelmingly positive, with many appreciating the efforts of Team ABHEDYA in organizing such an informative and engaging program.

The keynote speech by Mr. Sandeep Mudalkar, the CEO and founder of Sytechlabs Pvt.ltd, was a highlight of the event. His speech provided the students with a deeper understanding of the complexities involved in cybersecurity. The panel discussion was also informative, allowing the students to interact with experts in the field and gain insights into the challenges involved in safeguarding themselves against cyber threats

The event included a panel discussion as well, giving the students a chance to speak with professionals in the subject of cybersecurity. The panel discussion was thought-provoking and educational, and the students had the chance to ask questions and gain knowledge of the difficulties associated with protecting oneself from cyber-attacks. The panel discussion gave the students a fantastic chance to hear from business professionals about their real-world experiences dealing with cybercrime.

In conclusion, the CYBORG event was a well-organized and informative program that provided the students of VBIT with a comprehensive understanding of cybersecurity. The event was a great success, with distinguished guest speakers who shared their experiences and knowledge, making the event a huge success. Team ABHEDYA was appreciated for their efforts in organizing such an informative and engaging program. The event was a testament to the importance of spreading cyber awareness, and we hope to see more such events in the future.
